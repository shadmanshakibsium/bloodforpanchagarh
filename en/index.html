✅ Who Can Donate Blood:

  • Age: Anyone between 18–60 years old can donate.
  • Weight: Minimum 48 kg for men, 45 kg for women.
  • Medical history: Wait at least 1 year after recovering from jaundice, malaria, or blood-borne diseases.
  • Women: Pregnant or breastfeeding women should avoid donating blood.
  • Previous donation: Wait at least 4 months between donations.
  • After surgery: Avoid donating within 6 months of major surgery.

📌 Tip: Make sure you’re physically fit before donating blood. Consult a doctor if unsure.
